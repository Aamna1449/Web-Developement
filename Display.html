Display:Inline

Takes only space required by the element.No linebreaks before and after. Setting width/height/padding/margin not allowed.

Box1

Display:Block

Takes full space available in width and leaves a new line before and after the element.

Box2

Display:Inline-block

Similar to inline but setting width,height,padding and margin is allowed.Element can sit to next each other.

Box3

Display:none VS visibility:hidden

With display:none , the element is removed from the document flow. Its space is not blocked. With visibility:hidden , the element is hidden but its space is reserved.
